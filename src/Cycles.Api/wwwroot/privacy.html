Privacy

Effective 22 July 2026

What Cycles receives

When you sign in with Google, Cycles receives Google’s stable account identifiers, your verified email address, and the time of sign-in. Cycles does not receive your Google password.

Cycles also stores the orders and other gameplay activity you submit, together with the resulting game history.

Why it is used

Your verified email is used once to match an invitation to an existing Cycles player. Later sign-ins use Google’s issuer and subject identifiers so that the same person reaches the same player. Sign-in records support account security and operational auditing.

Cookies and services

Cycles uses secure, HTTP-only session cookies and an anti-forgery cookie to keep you signed in and protect submitted actions. The application is delivered through Cloudflare and Microsoft Azure, and sign-in is provided by Google. Identity data is not shown to other players, sold, or used for advertising.

Retention and access

Identity links are retained while the invited Cycles account remains active. Gameplay facts may be retained as part of the persistent Chronicle. To request access, correction, removal, or disconnection of an identity, contact the Cycles operator through the channel used for your invitation or the project owner’s GitHub profile.
