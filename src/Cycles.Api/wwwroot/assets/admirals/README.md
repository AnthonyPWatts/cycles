# Admiral Portrait Assets

This directory contains 35 unassigned admiral portrait concepts for future named-character presentation work:

- 18 human portraits;
- 17 non-human portraits;
- 1024 x 1024 WebP masters;
- four neutral background cohorts with space for later decorative or informational frames.

The original exploration targeted 36 portraits, but generation stopped after the current 35 had produced enough useful range. The missing portrait is one non-human member of the archive-violet cohort.

| Cohort | Accent | Symbol | Human | Non-human |
| --- | --- | --- | ---: | ---: |
| `astrolabe-gold` | Antique gold | Compass-star astrolabe | 5 | 4 |
| `gateway-teal` | Teal | Paired gateway rings | 4 | 5 |
| `spearpoint-crimson` | Crimson | Converging chevrons | 5 | 4 |
| `archive-violet` | Indigo-violet | Archive star | 4 | 4 |

The cohorts do not yet have gameplay meaning. They may later represent factions, aptitudes, rarity, doctrine, or another presentation category without requiring the files to be renamed.

[`catalogue.json`](catalogue.json) is the machine-readable index. Its portrait IDs are presentation keys, not character identities or names.
