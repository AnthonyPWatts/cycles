The premise

Win the Cycle. Leave a mark the galaxy cannot erase.

01

Project influence

Fleets create pressure rather than permanent borders. A system can be shared, contested, isolated, or pulled from a rival without becoming a painted tile.

02

Commit your intent

Set priorities, move fleets, establish outposts, and choose when cold pressure becomes open war. The next tick resolves every commitment.

03

Become history

Most actions change the map. A few become battles, legends, and famous systems that echo into the next Cycle.
